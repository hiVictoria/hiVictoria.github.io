Costar is revolutionizing the real estate marketplace by building a next-generation collaborative marketplace with Homes.com to help millions find their perfect dream home.

With a full redesign and relaunch of the product planned across native applications and responsive web, Homes.com aims to transform and redefine the home-buying experience by seamlessly allowing users to connect with agents & sellers and providing them with the right tools at every step of the process.

SUMMER 2022

I worked within Homes.com's cross-functional product team as a UX/UI Design Intern this past summer. I was lucky enough to enter the team at a point in time where Homes.com was in the midst of being completely redesigned. I got the chance to work on multiple projects and collaborate with project managers, directors, interns and researchers:

  • Designed and prototyped a new organizational feature for native app
  • Validated design decisions by conducting usability and A/B testing
  • Built a case study and pitched a new homes recommendation initiative
  • Standardized mobile & desktop mocks to be compliant with new design systems
  • Synthesized research on toast messages
  • Conducted research on sounds & haptics to present recommendations for incorporation into native app
  • Created empty state vector illustrations
